grid calendar

a single-page, full-year, row-wise, printable calendar.

a calendar that

  • shows you all the days of the year
  • shows you the holidays (if you want)
  • is

    free to print

  • looks nice

Me and Ashley used it to organize our year.


how to print your own copy

  1. download the version you want
  2. take it to staples/wherever
  3. print it large (preferably ~24" x 40")
  4. laminate it (optional)

how to use custom colors

if you'd rather use your own colors, use the configure page.

once you're happy, print it. For best results, adjust your print settings to landscape orientation and disable the header and footer.
